Resources for Educators

Huntington has been working with local schools and school districts for over thirty-five years, to provide tutoring solutions that respond to the needs of schools, students and their families.

We provide an individualized approach to every student. In addition to our proven tutoring and test prep programs, Huntington can also provide your students with Summer School Programs and College and Career Readiness Programs.

Huntington offers students the best private tutoring options to address:
Academic Skills
  • Programs for K-12 students
  • If your student is having difficulty with a subject or schoolwork in general, our Academic Skills tutoring program can help.
  • Reading • Writing • Study Skills • Math • Phonics • Spelling • Vocabulary
Subject Tutoring
  • Programs for Middle School - College students
  • Our programs are designed for students who want better grades, or who simply want to learn more.
  • Pre-algebra • Algebra • Geometry • Trigonometry • Pre-calculus • Calculus • Earth Science • Biology • Chemistry • Physics • and more!
Exam Prep
  • Whether for college entrance, scholarship, placement, high school entrance, or state tests, all Huntington Exam Prep programs start with a personalized one-to-one tutoring plan designed around a student's strengths and weaknesses to help the student achieve his or her test score goals.
  • SAT • PSAT • ACT • SAT II • Advanced Placement (AP) • State Tests • HSPT • ISEE • SSAT • GED • ASVAB • and more!
Other resources:
Homework Presentation
  • Huntington's "Parent's Survival Guide" Homework Presentation is a practical workshop, offering parents the advice they need to tackle common homework problems. Topics covered include sources of homework frustration, study space and learning atmosphere, homework expectations by grade level and common homework problems. Please call your local center to schedule a presentation.
Exam Prep Presentation
  • Huntington's ACT, SAT and PSAT* Presentation offers parents the advice they need to help their student prepare, achieve, and succeed on their college admissions exams. Topics covered include facts about the exams, strategies to improve scores and tips on how to prepare for the exams. Please call your local center to schedule a presentation.
Summer school programs
  • Huntington is accredited by the Middle States Commission on Elementary and Secondary Schools and is a wonderful option for high schools that do not offer their own summer school programs.
College and career readiness
  • Huntington partners with your school to implement College and Career Readiness Programs for your students.
Summer school programs

  • Career Opportunities
