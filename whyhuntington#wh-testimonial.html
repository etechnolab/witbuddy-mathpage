The Results Are In: Huntington Delivers

Huntington Learning Center is dedicated to not only meeting, but exceeding today's top education standards. Corporate level and regional accreditation is a symbol of our organization's commitment to integrity.

Why does accreditation matter to you? Just like you wouldn't send your child to a non-accredited college, you want to be able to trust that your student's tutors know what they are doing and are equipped to give the best help possible. Private tutors are not usually certified or accredited, but Huntington tutors are. You can trust that we know what we're doing and can help your child succeed, just like the thousands of other students we've helped before.

What is accreditation

Accreditation is the process by which groups of educational institutions and practitioners identify those institutions that meet or exceed commonly accepted standards of educational quality. Huntington Learning Centers, Inc. has been accredited by the Middle States Association of Colleges and Schools since 1996, and has been reaccredited every five years since then.

In a nutshell, accreditation certifies Huntington’s competency and credibility when it comes to tutoring programs.

A history of success ensuring a brighter future

more "I can" moments since 1977

First in the industry, Huntington has the longest track record of helping students achieve academic success and boost their confidence... we’ve been successfully helping kids learn for nearly four decades. Our mission to give every student the best education possible has been our mantra since the beginning, and we love to see students realize “I can” when it comes to school success. Named after the family that started it all, Huntington continues to be a family-owned company with its executives deeply involved in the day-to-day aspects of the business.

The first Huntington Learning Center

Dr. Raymond and Eileen Huntington break new ground in the tutoring industry by opening the first Huntington Learning Center in River Edge, New Jersey.

HUNTINGTON BEGINS TO GROW

Building on the success of the first center and wanting to give every student the best education possible, Huntington opens a second center in Livingston, New Jersey.

REFINING HUNTINGTON'S 4-STEP APPROACH

As Huntington grows, they continue to adapt their tutoring methods, expanding and formalizing what is known today as the four-step approach. Over the years, their four-step approach has seen proven results and helped tens of thousands of students.

HUNTINGTON EXPANDS NATIONALLY

With 18 centers in the northern New Jersey and Philadelphia areas, Huntington begins national expansion, opening the first franchised center in New Hampshire.

HUNTINGTON EMBRACES IMPROVED TECHNOLOGY

To provide improved tutoring services and better manage student information, Huntington begins development of a sophisticated computerized student management system.

HUNTINGTON MAKES THE GRADE

Huntington receives accreditation by Middle States Association and the Western Association of Schools and Colleges. Parents can trust that Huntington knows what they are doing when it comes to helping students achieve academic success.

HUNTINGTON CONTINUES GROWTH TO HELP MORE STUDENTS

Striving to help even more students achieve academic success, Huntington continues to add centers, reaching 148 franchised centers by 2000.

FIRST OFFERED SERVICES UNDER THE NO CHILD LEFT BEHIND ACT

In an effort to ensure students receive the education they need, President Bush signs the No Child Left Behind Act, and Huntington is there to offer services.

HUNTINGTON FOUNDERS RECEIVE RECOGNITION

Eileen and Ray Huntington receive the Education Industry Association’s Entrepreneurial Leadership Award for their "courage, perseverance, and determination to improve educational opportunities for children."

HUNTINGTON RECEIVES HIGH ACCOLADES

Huntington receives high accolades from a third-party study of its No Child Left Behind services in Chicago.

HUNTINGTON CONTINUES TO EXPAND TECHNOLOGY

Recognizing the ways advancing technologies are aiding modern students in learning, Huntington expands its computerized system to include a web-based learning management system.

HUNTINGTON HELPS ADHD STUDENTS

Huntington begins making its tutoring services available to students with ADHD through its partnership with a leading expert.
